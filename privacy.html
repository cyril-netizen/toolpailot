Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

At ToolPilot, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and tools at https://cyril-netizen.github.io/toolpailot (the "Site"). Our core principle is simple: your data never leaves your device.

1. Data Collection & Processing

Client-Side Processing Only

All ToolPilot tools run 100% in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • No data is sent to our servers for processing
  • No personal information is uploaded, stored, or analyzed by us
  • All tool operations occur on your device only
  • Your sensitive data (passwords, JSON, code, etc.) never leaves your computer

When you use any ToolPilot tool, whether it's the JSON formatter, password generator, hash creator, or any other utility, all processing happens locally on your device.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No tool input data or results
  • No behavioral analytics or usage tracking
  • No IP-based location data
  • No device fingerprinting

2. Cookies & Local Storage

Theme Preference

ToolPilot uses your browser's localStorage to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode). This setting is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

  • Purpose: Improve user experience by remembering your preferred appearance
  • Storage: Browser localStorage (expires when you clear browser data)
  • Data Collected: Only your theme choice ("light" or "dark")

Google AdSense Cookies

ToolPilot displays advertisements through Google AdSense. These cookies are only loaded after you give your explicit consent via the cookie banner displayed on your first visit. If you refuse, no advertising cookies are set and no ads are shown.

If you accept, Google uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Deliver targeted advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent ad fraud

These cookies are managed by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through Google Ad Settings. You can also change your consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to monetize the site. Google may collect information about your browsing activity to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's privacy policies, not ours.

Fonts

ToolPilot uses the Inter font, which is hosted locally on our servers. No request is made to Google or any third-party CDN to load fonts, ensuring your IP address is not shared with font providers.

GitHub Pages (Hosting Provider)

The ToolPilot website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses and page requests. See GitHub Pages data collection for details.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

ToolPilot does not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics tools
  • Heatmap tracking software
  • User session recording tools
  • Conversion tracking pixels
  • Behavioral profiling

We do not track how you use the tools, which features you access, or how long you spend on the site.

5. GDPR Compliance (EU Regulation 2016/679)

ToolPilot fully complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive:

  • Consent Before Cookies: No advertising or tracking cookies are set before you give your explicit consent via our cookie banner (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR, Article 5(3) ePrivacy Directive).
  • Data Minimization: We collect minimal data. The only information stored on your device is your theme preference and your cookie consent choice.
  • No Personal Data Processing: We do not process personal data from your tool usage. All tool processing happens in your browser.
  • Your Rights: Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
    • Access (Art. 15) — Request what data we hold about you.
    • Rectification (Art. 16) — Request correction of inaccurate data.
    • Erasure (Art. 17) — Request deletion of your data.
    • Restriction (Art. 18) — Request limitation of data processing.
    • Portability (Art. 20) — Receive your data in a portable format.
    • Objection (Art. 21) — Object to data processing.
    • Withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — Withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.
    Since ToolPilot does not collect personal data beyond localStorage preferences, most of these rights are satisfied by default. You can delete ToolPilot data at any time via your browser settings.
  • Third-Party Data: If you accept advertising cookies, Google processes data under their own privacy policies. You can exercise your rights regarding Google's data collection through Google Ad Settings and Google Account settings.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Essential cookies (theme preference, consent choice): Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — required for site functionality.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — only activated after explicit user consent.

Since all processing happens on your device:

ToolPilot uses a Service Worker to cache the website locally for offline access. This means:

  • The site and tools work offline after the first visit
  • Cache data is stored only on your device
  • No data is sent to servers for caching purposes

12. Your Responsibility

While ToolPilot protects your privacy by running all tools client-side, remember that:

  • Your browser history may show tools you've used
  • Your device's cache may contain cached pages
  • Anyone with access to your device can see your session history
  • We recommend clearing your browser history if using public or shared devices

Summary

ToolPilot is built on privacy-first principles:

  • All tools run on your device, not on our servers
  • We don't collect or store your tool data
  • We don't track your activity or behavior
  • Advertising cookies require your explicit consent before loading
  • Fonts are hosted locally — no third-party font CDN requests
  • Only your theme preference and cookie consent choice are stored locally

Your data is your data. ToolPilot simply provides the tools—the processing stays on your device.

About Password Generator

Our free password generator helps you create strong, secure passwords for all your online accounts. In an era of increasing cyber threats, using unique, complex passwords is one of the most important steps you can take to protect your personal information.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Weak passwords are vulnerable to brute-force attacks and dictionary attacks. By generating random passwords with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols, you significantly increase the difficulty for attackers to compromise your accounts. Our password generator creates cryptographically random passwords that meet security best practices.

Features

  • Customizable Length: Generate passwords from 8 to 128 characters
  • Character Selection: Include or exclude uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Strength Meter: See real-time password strength assessment
  • Bulk Generation: Create multiple passwords at once
  • One-Click Copy: Easily copy passwords to your clipboard
  • 100% Client-Side: All generation happens in your browser—no data is sent to any server

Password Security Tips

  • Use at least 12 characters for important accounts
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Never reuse passwords across different accounts
  • Use a password manager to securely store your passwords
  • Change passwords regularly and immediately if compromised
  • Avoid using personal information like birthdates or names

How to Create Strong Passwords

Creating strong passwords is critical for account security. A secure password creator like ours helps by combining multiple character types: uppercase letters (A-Z), lowercase letters (a-z), numbers (0-9), and special symbols (!@#$%^&*). The longer your password, the more difficult it becomes to crack. Our tool lets you generate passwords from 8 to 128 characters, giving you complete control over password strength and complexity.

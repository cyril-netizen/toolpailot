What is a QR Code?

QR codes (Quick Response codes) are two-dimensional barcodes that can store text, URLs, contact information, and more. They can be scanned by smartphones and other devices to quickly access information. QR codes are widely used in marketing, event tickets, product packaging, and business cards.

How to Use the QR Code Generator

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use generated QR codes commercially?

Yes, absolutely. QR codes are freely generated and can be used for any purpose, commercial or personal. No licensing or attribution is required.

What data can QR codes store?

QR codes can store URLs, plain text, contact information (vCard), WiFi credentials, email addresses, phone numbers, and more. Our tool supports any text up to 4,296 characters.

Is my data safe with this QR code generator?

Yes, all processing happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your information remains completely private and secure.

What image formats can I download?

Currently, QR codes are exported as PNG images, which offer lossless compression and transparency support. PNG is ideal for both digital and print use.

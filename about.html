About ToolPilot

Alex Devane
Full-Stack Developer & ToolPilot Creator

ToolPilot was born out of frustration. After years of bouncing between bloated tool websites that demand email signups, track your every move, and slow down because of tracking scripts, I decided enough was enough. I wanted to build a place where developers could access useful tools without the baggage — no sign-ups, no tracking, just pure functionality.

With over 8 years of experience building web applications and contributing to open-source projects, I've always believed that great software doesn't need to be complicated or invasive. ToolPilot embodies a privacy-first philosophy: everything runs on your browser, nothing is sent to servers, and there's zero data collection. You own your work entirely.

My background spans full-stack development across various industries — from startups to established companies across Europe. I've seen how good developer tooling can save countless hours, and poor tooling can drain your productivity. This experience shaped ToolPilot's design: fast, intuitive, and respectful of your time and privacy.

The mission is simple: keep ToolPilot free, fast, and valuable. It's supported by unobtrusive ads, not by selling your data or locking features behind paywalls. If you find ToolPilot useful, consider supporting the project — it takes time and passion to maintain quality tools for the community.
